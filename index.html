Project Overview:

Why is this project important?

Exercise is a cornerstone of maintaining oneâ€™s health, both physically and mentally. GymPet helps its users form and maintain their physical exercise habits, which can have a positive impact in their lives all-around!

Gym Pet allows you to customize and create a virtual pet to help with your exercise goals. Pets can communicate with you through affirmations before, during, and after workout sessions. We also have a journaling component where you can share thoughts, feelings, and notes throughout your personal journeys.

To maintain routines and goals, you can earn tokens each time you workout. Tokens can be used to purchase customizable items like clothing and accessories.

Some additional app features include reminders and sharing workout routines with friends. You can link workout sessions with your friends, and have your virtual pets interact as well!

Design Phases

Brain Storm

Starting off, we knew we wanted to create an app that held people accountable for their health while also positively enforcing healthy behaviors. We settled on an app that would allow people to track their workouts and would keep them working out by implementing a cute incentive!

Sketches

After deciding, we began drafting up ideas on what the virtual pets would look like, how they would interact with users, and we decided on other features that would make our app different from others. We made sure to send out surveys to better understand what our audience is looking for.

Low-fi prototype

We then created the low-fi figma based on these ideas to see how our app would function. the data we collected from this allowed us to make revisions to better suit the needs of our consumers. Finally, after revisions were made, we ended up with a final product that we are all very proud of!
